Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my content?

Scan the QR code displayed on Contact Us page, you will then fill out a form. Once you completed it, the Canister team will reach out to you with instructions on how to proceed with uploading your projects onto the platform. The submission process for films can be done either by providing a downloadable MP4 file or by sharing a link to your YouTube video. The Canister team will guide you through the necessary steps to ensure your films are successfully uploaded and shared on the platform.

As a content creator, do I receive revenue by uploading on the website?

No content creator receives any type of revenue by uploading content on a Canister.

Is Canister making any profit from my ShortFilms?

Canister is a non-profit organization with the sole objective of sharing students' works and allowing them to reach new audiences. As a non-profit platform, Canister does not generate any profit from your short films and operates solely to support and showcase the creative endeavors of Full Sail University students.

Is the platform limited to networking only for Full Sail University?

Yes, the platform is primarily focused on networking for Full Sail University students. However, it may also provide opportunities for showcasing and promoting their short films beyond the college community.

If I have projects not related to movies, can I upload them to Canister?

As of right now, Canister is primarily focused on films created by Full Sail University students. However, while currently limited to films, Canister has aspirations to become a broader platform that showcases various creative projects produced by Full Sail University students.
